# SIM D — Institutional Financial Mechanics

## Purpose
SIM D introduces institutional financial mechanisms observed in insurance markets.

## Institutional Layer Introduced
Institutional financial governance.

## Model Structure

- underwriting agents
- capital resources
- tax environment
- reinsurance structures

## Key Mechanisms

- corporate taxation
- deferred tax assets
- quota share reinsurance
- excess of loss reinsurance
- dividend policy
- cyclical premium environments

## Simulation Environment

- multi-period underwriting cycles
- stochastic market conditions

## Outcome Metrics

- capital stability
- underwriting profitability
- institutional resilience

## Relationship to Previous Simulation

SIM D extends SIM C by incorporating financial governance mechanisms.

## Notes

These additions increase the realism of the underwriting institution.
