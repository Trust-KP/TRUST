# SIM H — Capital Scaling Dynamics

## Purpose
SIM H explores the effects of large-scale capital accumulation on
insurance market dynamics.

## Institutional Layer Introduced
Capital scaling and shareholder incentive dynamics.

## Model Structure

- underwriting agents
- large capital expansion regimes
- reinvestment strategies

## Key Mechanisms

- capital reinvestment policies
- market share expansion
- capital drawdown under stress

## Simulation Environment

- multi-agent insurance market
- stochastic boom–bust cycles

## Outcome Metrics

- market concentration
- capital accumulation
- institutional resilience

## Relationship to Previous Simulation

SIM H extends SIM G by introducing extreme capital scaling regimes.

## Notes

This stage explores how capital structure influences long-term market
dominance.
